About the Project

In this project, we follow Alex, a high school senior who will choose to either purchase a car right away, or to save for a car for 6 months in hopes to buy a car. Integrating case-based learning with high degree of relevancy, I integrate elements of real-life into this experience. Motivational mechanics, such as a net-worth bar is also included not only to motivate the learner to make saving choices, but also to help them keep track of their progress.

  • Target Audience

    High school seniors and adults who are struggling to see the benefits of saving.

  • Learning Objective

    Students will create and calculate for their own use cases of the compound interest formula.

  • ID Skills

    Action Mapping, Storyboarding, Vyond and Articulate Storyline

Development Process

Once I had my low-fidelity mockup, I began creating visuals and videos on Vyond Studio. After setting my color theme and finalizing a few scenes, I moved on to creating a high-fidelity mockup. The creation of videos and the high-fidelity mockup happened simultaneously.

Once the high-fidelity mockup and videos were completed, I downloaded all the videos and began production in Storyline. The project became quite extensive, offering learners autonomy as they progressed through the experience. Here’s a demonstration of how the final project looked in Storyline.
